StockGenie

Lightweight stock analysis & portfolio helper

What is StockGenie?

StockGenie is a compact web tool for hobby traders who want quick stock insights, portfolio tracking, and simple visualizations without the clutter of enterprise platforms.

Key Features

  • Quick stock lookups and charting
  • Simple portfolio tracking
  • Lightweight, fast UI focused on usability
